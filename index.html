Our Donate Plan

We have a variety of plans for donations throughout the year. These plans are currently
being implemented transparently in our campaign.

Food for a family

Every day people in different areas go hungry. Due to various natural disasters or social unrest, we want to help people who are starving. If you want to join it, join our campaign.

Shelter for a family

Every year due to various natural disasters in our country, people's homes are destroyed and they do not have a place to live or a house of their own. We have launched this campaign to help these people.

Education for a child

When a disaster strikes in an area, the education system in that area goes to the brink of destruction. In order to prevent destruction, we provide education to the children. The campaign is for those who are interested in teaching children.
